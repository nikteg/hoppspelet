How to play

  • 🕹️ Jump with SPACEBAR – or tap anywhere on the screen.
  • ⚠️ Jump over obstacles and squeeze under hanging blocks. Touching anything ends the round!
  • 🪙 Collect coins – each coin gives 10 points, and you earn points for how far you run.
  • 🌍 Every 1000 points you enter a new world – there are 65 different ones!
  • 💨 Speed increases the further you go. How far can you make it?
